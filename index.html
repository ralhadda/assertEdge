About Assert Edge

AssertEdge provides specialized test automation services tailored for healthcare startups. With over five years of hands-on experience in test automation within the healthcare industry, I bring deep technical expertise and an understanding of the unique challenges healthcare technology faces. Currently, I am the sole operator driving AssertEdge, dedicated to delivering high-quality, reliable, and scalable automation solutions using Cypress and other modern tools. While starting as a one-person operation, AssertEdge is built with growth in mind — ready to expand and support healthcare startups at every stage as their automation needs evolve. My mission is to empower healthcare innovators by ensuring their software is robust, compliant, and ready to scale, so they can focus on what matters most: improving patient outcomes.
